# HR Onboarding Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

*For HR Team Members*

---

## What This Tool Does

Manages the entire new hire onboarding process. Add a new employee, generate all their onboarding documents (offer letter, IT request, facilities request, manager checklist, welcome email) with one click, and track their progress through a visual dashboard.

---

## How to Access It

Open this link in your browser:

**https://webapp-nine-rho-64.vercel.app**

No login required. Works on any computer with internet access.

---

## How to Use It

### Adding a New Hire

1. Click **"+ New Hire"** (top right corner)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Employee Name** (required)
   - **Position** (required)
   - **Department** (required - pick from the dropdown)
   - **Manager** (optional but recommended)
   - **Start Date** (optional but recommended)
   - **Annual Salary** (optional)
   - **Email** (leave blank and it creates one automatically: first.last@precisionmfg.com)
3. Click **"Add Employee"**

The new hire appears as a card on the dashboard.

### Generating Onboarding Documents

1. Find the employee's card on the dashboard
2. Click **"Generate Documents"**
3. All 5 documents are created instantly
4. A popup shows you each document. Click the tabs to switch between them:
   - Offer Letter
   - IT Setup Request
   - Facilities Request
   - Manager Prep Checklist
   - Welcome Email
5. Copy and paste each document where needed (email, print, etc.)

### Tracking Progress

Each employee card shows:
- **Status badge** (color-coded: yellow = pending, blue = in progress, green = complete)
- **Progress bar** (fills up as checkboxes are completed)
- **Checklist badges** showing which steps are done

Click **"Advance Status"** to move them to the next stage:

Offer Pending -> Offer Sent -> Offer Signed -> IT Setup -> Onboarding -> Complete

### Viewing Documents Again

Click **"View Documents"** on any card to see their generated documents again.

### Deleting a Record

Click **"Delete"** on the card. You'll be asked to confirm before it's removed.

### Refreshing Data

Click **"Refresh"** (top right) to pull the latest data from Airtable.

---

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

**Dashboard shows "Loading..." and nothing appears**
Refresh the page. If still loading, check your internet connection. Try again in a few minutes.

**New hire doesn't appear after adding**
Click "Refresh". The card should appear. If not, check Airtable directly to see if the record was created.

**Documents show "TBD" in some places**
Missing information in the employee record. Go back and fill in the missing fields (start date, manager, salary), then regenerate documents.

**Can't find an employee**
Scroll down - cards are sorted by status (pending first, then in-progress, then complete).
